# HyperMenu
* HyperMenu is an easy-to-use Among Us cheat menu with a simple GUI and lots of useful modules, based on MalumMenu by scp222thj and astra1dev.

# About HyperMenu
* HyperMenu was developed by ADHyperActive.
* MalumMenu, which HyperMenu was forked from, was developed by scp222thj and astra1dev.
* Credit goes to the developers of MalumMenu.
